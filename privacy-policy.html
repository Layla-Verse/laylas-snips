Layla's Snips Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Layla's Snips does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind.

All snippet content and extension settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built in chrome.storage.local API.

The extension does not:

  • collect keystrokes
  • track browsing activity
  • send data to external servers
  • use analytics or tracking scripts
  • use cookies

All extension functionality happens inside your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere. If you uninstall the extension, all stored data is automatically removed by Chrome.
