Layla's Snips – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Layla’s Snips is designed with user privacy as its highest priority. This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All functionality takes place entirely within your browser.

What Data Layla’s Snips Stores

Layla’s Snips stores only the snippet data that you create (trigger phrases, handles, and body text). This information is saved locally on your device using Chrome’s chrome.storage.local API.

No snippet data is ever uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any external servers, services, or third parties.

Permissions

"<all_urls>" Permission

Layla’s Snips requires the "<all_urls>" permission so that it can detect your trigger phrases inside text fields across all websites. This allows the extension to function in:

  • standard text inputs
  • textareas
  • rich-text editors
  • embedded text fields inside iframes
  • enterprise platforms such as Salesforce, Smartsheet, LIMS, and Google Sheets

Although the extension can access these pages to detect trigger phrases, it does not read, store, transmit, or log any website content or keystrokes. All processing happens locally within the browser.

What the Extension Does Not Do

The extension does not:

  • collect or record keystrokes
  • track browsing activity
  • capture or upload webpage content
  • send data to external servers
  • use analytics, trackers, or cookies
  • share data with the developer or third parties

Data Removal

All snippet data is stored solely on your device. If you uninstall the extension, all stored data is automatically deleted by Chrome.
